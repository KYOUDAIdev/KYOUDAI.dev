## What is Joy?

Joy is not happiness. Happiness is a reaction to external circumstances. Joy is a state of beingâ€”a telos, an end goal that shapes every decision.

When we say "with joy as our telos," we mean:

> Every line of code, every design decision, every interaction should move us closer to a state of flow, delight, and human flourishing.

## Joy in Development

Traditional software development optimizes for:
- Speed
- Efficiency  
- Scalability
- Profit

But what if we optimized for **joy**?

### The Joy Stack

1. **Playfulness** - Embrace experimentation
2. **Clarity** - Reduce cognitive load
3. **Delight** - Surprise and enchant
4. **Flow** - Remove friction
5. **Meaning** - Connect to purpose

## Practice

Ask yourself:

1. Does this feature bring joy?
2. Does it reduce friction or add it?
3. Will users smile when they use it?
4. Am I having fun building it?

If the answer is "no," reconsider.
